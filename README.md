# TI-BH-basic-kit

This is a basic kit that includes two different kinds of TI Launchpad boards and jumper wires.  <br />
Students who take ESET 369 course in *Spring of 2019* need to bring their own TI Launchpad boards and jumper wires. <br />

The detailed items were listed below. Students can choose to obtain them from several vendors such as TI, Amazon, digikey, or mouser. <br />
